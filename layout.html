Foundations

Layout

A 4px spacing scale, a three-step rhythm, a 1200px content column, and five breakpoints.

Spacing#

Everything sits on a 4px base (spacing-base). The named steps multiply it: 4, 8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 40, 64, 96.

spacing-14pxTightest gaps - icon to label
spacing-28pxInside a group
spacing-312pxCompact padding
spacing-416pxBetween groups; compact cards
spacing-624pxCard padding
spacing-832pxBetween sections
spacing-1040pxLarge section breaks
spacing-1664pxPage-level air
spacing-2496pxHero spacing

Rhythm#

The three-step rhythm

  • 8px between elements inside a group.
  • 16px between groups.
  • 32-40px between sections.
  • Cards take 24px padding - 16px when compact, 32px for hero areas.

Container#

Content centers in a 1200px column, with side padding that grows at wider breakpoints. Every layout must hold up on both mobile and desktop.
