Foundations

Colors

Ten-step scales where every step has a job. Solid gray for text and fills, translucent gray-alpha for borders and overlays, and seven accent scales that carry meaning.

How steps work#

Each non-background scale runs ten steps, 100 to 1000. A step is a role, not just a lightness: backgrounds live at the bottom, borders in the middle, fills and text at the top. Hover and active states move one step up.

StepRole
100Default background
200Hover background
300Active background
400Default border
500Hover border
600Active border
700Solid fill, high contrast
800Solid fill, hover
900Secondary text and icons
1000Primary text and icons
