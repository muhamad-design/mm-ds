Foundations

Motion

Instant by default. When motion earns its place, it is short, physical, and rides a single curve.

Principles#

Motion exists to clarify a change, never to decorate. Most interactions read best instantly - 0ms is a valid duration and often the right one. When something genuinely moves or reveals, keep it short and physical.

Durations#

instant0ms0The default. Most state changes read best with no transition at all.
state150msSmall state changes - hover, press, selection.
popover200msPopovers, tooltips, dropdown menus.
overlay300msOverlays, modals, drawers.

Easing#

ease
One springy curve for everything that moves - a fast start with a slight overshoot at the end.

Reduced motion#

Always honor the user

  • Respect prefers-reduced-motion: drop every nonessential animation.
  • No loops, no attention-grabbing movement.
