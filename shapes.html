Foundations

Shapes

Tight radii - 6, 12, 16, and full - matched to surface size, one family per view.

Radii#

Radii stay tight and consistent.

rounded-sm6px
Everyday surfaces - buttons, inputs, cards.
rounded-md12px
Menus and modals.
rounded-lg16px
Fullscreen and near-fullscreen surfaces.
rounded-full9999px
Pills, avatars, and circular controls.

Rules#

Keep corners coherent

  • One radius family per view - never mix rounded and sharp corners.
  • Bigger surface, bigger radius: 6px controls, 12px menus and modals, 16px fullscreen.
  • rounded-full is reserved for pills, avatars, and circles.
